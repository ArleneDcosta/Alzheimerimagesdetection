What are the Symptoms?
Memory loss is the key symptom of Alzheimer's disease. An early sign of the disease is usually difficulty remembering recent events or conversations. As the disease progresses, memory impairments worsen and other symptoms develop.

How can Alzheimers be treated?
There is no cure or treatment as such for Alzheimers.Some drugs and medicines are introduced by the US govt that helps individuals living with the disease and their caregivers to cope with symptoms and improve quality of life.
